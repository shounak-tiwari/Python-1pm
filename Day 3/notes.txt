binary operators : 
when we want to perform operations between bits use binary of any decimals like 15-> 1111 and 53 -> 110101

in python, binary is calculate using bin() methods


binary operators are : 

at least two decimals bit required : 

1. and & 
2. or  | 
3. xor ^

only one decimals bit required  : 

4. right-shift >>
5. left-shift  <<
6. compliment  ~
